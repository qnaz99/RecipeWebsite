Maki Sushi

by Qareeb

Posted on December 1, 2020 at 2:06 PM

Maki Sushi is what most people have in mind when they say sushi.

This recipe teaches you to make authentic maki roll that you can then fill with whatever fillings your heart desires.

Feel free to get creative with it and try your own fillings.

Ingredients:

Servings: 4

MAKI SUSHI
100g sushi rice (see Step 1)
1 sheet nori seaweed
2 tbsp sushi vinegar
soy sauce (to taste)
wasabi (to taste)
sushi ginger (to taste)
roasted white sesame seeds (optional)

POSSIBLE FILLINGS
tuna/salmon â€“ sashimi grade, raw
avocado
cucumuber
prawns

Instructions:

Step 1: Mix Japanese white rice with sushi rice vinegar to make sushi rice.
Step 2: Place a sheet on your makisu rolling mat and cover 2 thirds of one side of it with nori seaweed with your sushi rice 1cm high
Step 3: Add your chosen filling in a line on top of the rice in the center. For example, you can use salmon, salad and mayonaise for a classic tasty combo.
Step 4: Using the wooden rolling mat, start rolling up the ingredients away from you while keeping the roll tight
Step 5: Cut the roll into 6-8 pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, giner and a cup of green tea.
