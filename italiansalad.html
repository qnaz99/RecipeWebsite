Italian Salad

by Qareeb

Posted on March 31, 2019 at 10:39 AM

Homemade Italian salad only takes a few minutes and tastes incredible.

It's so easy to get creative with it and make it looks as good as it tastes.

Plus, with the right ingredient choice, you can make it super healthy. For example, you can change things up a bit and add some chicken for protein.

Ingredients:

Servings: 8

PANNA COTTA
8 cups lettuce (romaine or iceberg)
2 cups radicchio
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
¼ red onion, thinly sliced
1 cup seasoned croutons
½ cup black olives (pitted)
6 pepperoncini peppers
¼ cup parmesan cheese (optional)
1 tablespoon fresh herbs (parsley or basil)

DRESSING
1/2 cup vegetable/olive oil
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning (can be homemade)
1 teaspoon sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon/1 clove garlic (optional)

Instructions:

Step 1: Wash and dry the lettuce and radicchio and tear into pieces.
Step 2: Combine with the rest of the salad ingredients.
Step 3: Simply mix dressing ingredients together to make dressing.
Step 4: Toss everything together and serve.
