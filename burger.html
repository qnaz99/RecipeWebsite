Burger

by Judy

Posted on June 9, 2020 at 6:09 PM

Don't let the pandemic stop you from eating your summer favourites!

With this burger recipe you're sure to satisfy your cravings for not just this summer but for years to come.

Ingredients:

Servings: 12

1 lb. medium ground beef
1 egg
1 teaspoon mustard (regular or Dijon)
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 small onion, finely grated
1 clove garlic, minced
1⁄2 teaspoon salt
1⁄2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions:

Step 1: Lightly oil grill & heat BBQ to medium.
Step 2: Whisk egg in a bowl & add next 6 ingredients.
Step 3: You can add extra flavour such as curry or lemon powder, thyme, ketchup/BBQ/teriyaki sauce, salad dressing, etc. to make your burgers taste just they way you like them.
Step 4: Using your hands or a fork, crumble in the beef. Handle it as little as possible to prevent it from getting tough.
Step 5: Gently shape the burgers (do not press hard).
Step 6: Make a small depression in the center of each burger to prevent them from puffing up when grilling.
Step 7: Grill burgers until no longer pink inside (around 7 mins), flip them once. You can also use a meat thermometer (160 degrees F should be about right depending on how you like them).
Step 8: Place burger in a warm bun, add toppings and condiments and enjoy.
