Delicious Chicken Ramen

by Andrew

Posted on August 6, 2020 at 12:32 PM

This homemade chicken ramen recipe is simple, but delicious. Try it out!

Made with common household ingredients and just as flavorful as a pork broth!

Ingredients:

Serving: 4

CHICKEN RAMEN
2 chicken breasts
sea salt and ground pepper, to season
1 tbsp unsalted butter
2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
3 tsp fresh garlic, minced
3 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
2 tbsp mirin
4 cups chicken stock
1 oz dried shitake mushrooms
1-2 tsp sea salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup scallions, sliced
2 (3 oz) packs of ramen noodles

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190.5 degrees C).
Step 2: Season chicken with salt and pepper and melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Add chicken to skillet and cook until skin is brown (approx 5-7 mins). Flip chicken and cook another 4-5 mins. Put skillet in oven until chicken is cooked (approx 15-20 mins).
Step 3: Time to make the broth! Heat oil in a large pot until simmering. Add garlic and ginger and cook until soft. Add soy sauce and mirin to party and let the flavors blend together for a minute. Finally, add the stock and bring to a boil. After boiling, remove lid and simmer for 5-7 minutes before adding shitakes. Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes and season with salt.
Step 4: Make some delicious eggs! Add water to a pot and boil. Place eggs carefully into pot and simmer for 7-9 minutes.
Step 5: Let's chill them! Fill a bowl with ice cold water. When eggs finish cooking, place eggs into bowl to cool. After 5 or so minutes, peel eggs and slice.
Step 6: Time to assemble! Slice chicken into pieces. Cook ramen noodles in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Finally, assemble ramen bowls by placing noodles, broth and finally eggs in order. Top with any garnishes you like!
