Panna Cotta

by Ricardo

Posted on March 30, 2019 at 7:09 AM

This Italian desert is an easy go-to when you're short on time.

Not to mention it's creamy and delicous.

You can also have fun with it and top it off with colorful fruits and nuts. A great way to get the kids involved in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

Servings: 8

PANNA COTTA
1 envelope unflavored gelatin (about 1 tablespoon)
2 tablespoons cold water
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup half and half
1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

POSSIBLE TOPPINGS
blueberries, raspberries, chopped strawberries, etc.
walnuts, pecans, pistachios

Instructions:

Step 1: Sprinkle geletain over water in small saucepan and let stand for a 1 minute to soften.
Step 2: Combine cream, half and half, and sugar and bring to boil in a large saucepan over mid-high heat. Stir.
Step 3: Remove pan from heat and stir in gelatin mixture and vanilla.
Step 4: Divide mixture into 8 portions in small glasses or ramekins. Cool to room temperature, and then chill overnight.
Step 5: Dip glasses/ramekins into hot water for 3 secconds, then run a thin knife around the edge and turn upside-down onto center of a small plate to serve.
