Choco Mousse

by Qareeb

Posted on January 1, 2019 at 12:00 PM

Impress your guests this holiday season with a chocolote mousse that isn't just super easy to make, but incredibly delicious.


Seriously, you can't go wrong with this chocoloate mousse recipe.

Whether you're a last minute maniac or you've been planning the perfect party for months, the simplicity and brilliance of this recipe will keep everyone craving your next party.

Ingredients:

Servings: 6

CHOCO MOUSSE
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
6 ounces semisweet chocolate
3 large eggs, yolks and whites separated
1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 cup heavy cream, cold
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

WHEN SERVING
2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 cup heavy cream, cold
Chocolate shavings

Instructions:

Step 1: Microwave chocolate and butter in 20 second intervals until chocolate is 70% melted, then stir until fully melted. Allow it to cool.
Step 2: Whisk egg yolks in and mix until smooth.
Step 3: Seperately, beat egg whites until foamy with electric mixer. Add cream of tartar until soft peaks form.
Step 4: Beat in the 1/4 cup of sugar until smooth with peaks. Then combine the 2 mixtures by folding until uniform.
Step 5: Seperately, beat heavy cream until it begins to thicken. Add remaining sugar and vanilla.
Step 6: Fold it into the chocolate mixture. Don't mix more than necessary.
Step 7: Divide mousse into 6 serving glasses, cover them and chill for 2 hours.
Step 8: Before serving, whip cream until thick and add sugar. Add dollops onto the mousse and top with chocolate shavings (see picture).
